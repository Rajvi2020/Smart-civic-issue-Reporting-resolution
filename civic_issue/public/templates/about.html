About Us

Smart Traffic & Parking Assistance is a community-driven platform that helps citizens, departments and administrators reduce traffic congestion and improve parking management through real-time reporting.

Our Mission

Enable citizens to report traffic issues and help authorities resolve them quickly using live data and collaboration.

Our Vision

Build smarter, safer cities with efficient traffic flow, transparent reporting and better parking availability.

How It Works

1. Report

Citizen reports an issue.

2. Verify

Department verifies it.

3. Resolve

Action is taken.

4. Improve

Analytics improve planning.
